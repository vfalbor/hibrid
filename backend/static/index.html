How it routes

Three decisions, all automatic. A skill can declare its task type; if it doesn't, hibrid infers it. The response always tells you what it decided and why.

⚙Hardware-aware

Detects RAM, VRAM and chip, then micro-benchmarks the real tokens/sec of your models. It measures your machine instead of trusting a table.

↻Loops stay local

A refinement or QA loop is hundreds of cheap calls. hibrid keeps them on local models and saves the expensive cloud model for the one final check.

🔒Privacy by default

Detected personal data forces local execution. It's a rule, not a setting — your text never reaches a third party.

{}Drop-in

An OpenAI-compatible API. Adopting hibrid is changing the base URL. Nothing else in your code moves.

The gap it fills

Cloud gateways ignore your hardware. Local apps don't route to the cloud on their own. hibrid is the crossing of the two.
